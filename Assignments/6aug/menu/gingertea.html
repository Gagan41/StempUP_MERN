Ginger Tea

Ginger tea is a herbal beverage made from ginger root. It has a long history as a traditional herbal medicine in East Asia, South Asia, Southeast Asia, and West Asia.

In India, ginger tea is known as Adrak ki chai and is a widely consumed beverage. It is made by grating ginger into brewed black tea along with milk and sugar.

The beverage is known for health benefits like boosting immunity, relieving nausea, and reducing inflammation.
