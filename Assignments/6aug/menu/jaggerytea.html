Jaggery tea

Jaggery tea is also known as "gud ki chai", is a popular beverage, particularly in Maharashtra and Karnataka, where it's gaining popularity due to its potential health benefits and unique flavor.


It's made by adding jaggery (a type of unrefined sugar) to tea, along with spices like ginger and cardamom. Jaggery, derived from sugarcane or palm sap, offers a different sweetness profile compared to refined sugar and is believed to have various health advantages.While jaggery is a natural sweetener, it's still a source of carbohydrates and calories, so portion control is important, especially for those managing blood sugar levels or weight.
