Lemon tea

Lemon teais a beverage made by adding lemon to a tea base, usually black or green tea. It is a popular drink enjoyed for its flavor and potential health benefits. Lemon tea is sometimes consumed for its purported detoxifying properties and as a source of vitamins and minerals.


It is a source of Vitamin C, an antioxidant that can boost the immune system and support skin health, according to Apollo 24|7.
